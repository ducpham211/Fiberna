Fiberna

Founded in 2025, Fiberna is a pioneering manufacturer and exporter of eco-friendly packaging solutions,specializing in paper bags crafted from banana fiber—a sustainable alternative to traditional paper. By upcycling agricultural waste, we reduce environmental impact while promoting a circular economy.Our products meet international quality and environmental standards, and our commitment to traceability, production scalability, and global partnerships ensures dependable and ethical supply. At Fiberna, we believe innovation and sustainability can go hand in hand to deliver packaging that protects both your products and the planet.
